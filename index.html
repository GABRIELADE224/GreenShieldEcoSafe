Who We Are

01 — Introduction & Overview

GreenShield EcoSafe Solutions Limited is a professionally driven and innovative Environmental, Health, Safety, and Sustainability (EHSS) company providing integrated solutions to organizations across the public and private sectors in Nigeria.

As an indigenous company committed to excellence and global best practices, we deliver practical, sustainable, and regulatory-compliant solutions that address environmental challenges, enhance workplace safety, and promote public health. We assess client operations, evaluate compliance status, and provide tailored solutions aligned with Nigerian regulations and internationally recognized standards.

GreenShield is a multidisciplinary environmental, HSE and waste management consultancy headquartered in Ogun State, equipped to execute projects under diverse environmental and operational conditions — combining technical expertise with innovative strategies to deliver optimal results.

Our multidisciplinary approach ensures that every project is executed with precision, professionalism, and measurable impact.

Service Portfolio

06 — Seventeen Capabilities

We provide a comprehensive range of Environmental and HSE services — click any line to read the scope.

6.1Environmental & Social Impact Assessment (ESIA/EIA)+

Conducting detailed assessments of proposed projects to identify potential environmental impacts and recommend mitigation measures in line with regulatory requirements.

6.2Occupational Health & Safety (OHS) Management+

Development and implementation of safety management systems, hazard identification (HAZID), risk assessments, safety audits, and workplace inspections.

6.3Waste Management Services+

Provision of integrated waste management solutions, including waste collection, segregation, recycling, treatment, and environmentally safe disposal.

6.4Pollution Control & Environmental Protection+

Monitoring and mitigation of environmental pollution affecting air, water, and soil through scientifically proven and sustainable methods.

6.5Environmental Auditing & Compliance+

Assessment of organizational compliance with environmental laws, policies, and international standards.

6.6Emergency Response & Spill Management+

Rapid response to environmental incidents, including hazardous spills, industrial accidents, and remediation activities.

6.7Training & Capacity Building+

Delivery of professional training programs, workshops, and seminars in environmental management, occupational safety, and regulatory compliance.

6.8Environmental Monitoring & Evaluation+

Continuous monitoring of environmental parameters to ensure compliance and sustainable practices.

6.9Health Consultancy Services+

Provision of expert advisory services in environmental health, industrial hygiene, and workplace wellness.

6.10Supply of Safety Equipment+

Supply of high-quality Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) and safety tools that meet industry standards.

6.11Research & Development (R&D)+

Implementation of innovative research initiatives to improve environmental management systems and safety practices.

6.12Regulatory Compliance Support+

Support services including documentation, compliance audits, reporting, and liaison with regulatory authorities.

6.13General Contracts & Strategic Partnerships+

Execution of environmental and safety-related projects in collaboration with government agencies, NGOs, and private sector organizations.

6.14Disinfection, Disinfestation & Fumigation+

Comprehensive services designed to eliminate harmful microorganisms, insects, and rodents, covering residential, commercial, and industrial facilities including offices, healthcare institutions, and public spaces — using approved, environmentally friendly chemicals and modern application techniques.

6.15Landscape Construction & Management+

Landscape design, development of gardens and green areas, tree planting, installation of irrigation systems, and routine maintenance — combining environmental expertise with innovative design.

6.16Sewage & Sludge Evacuation Services+

Evacuation of septic tanks and soakaways, sludge removal, and proper disposal in compliance with environmental regulations, using modern equipment and best practices.

6.17Professional Cleaning Services+

Routine cleaning, deep cleaning, post-construction cleaning, and sanitation services using trained personnel, modern equipment, and safe cleaning agents.
